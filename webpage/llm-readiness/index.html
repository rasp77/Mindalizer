The Rise of Zero-Click Searches

AI Overviews appear in 6 out of 10 searches — halving click-through rates as search engines answer queries directly on the results page.

Synthesized AI Responses

ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini combine multiple sources into one answer — making individual website visits redundant for most informational queries.

Shifting User Behaviour

Users increasingly turn to AI for fast, direct answers — eliminating the need for manual research across multiple sites.

A Compressed, High-Stakes Landscape

AI cites only 2–7 domains per response — sites ranking well for humans may be entirely invisible to AI without proper content structure.

The strategic opportunity

Lower volume, far higher intent

Users who arrive via an AI citation convert at up to 3× the rate of traditional search visitors — they arrive with more context and higher intent. In the AI era, visibility means being included, cited, and recommended within AI responses. The businesses that optimise for this now will own the next decade of search.
